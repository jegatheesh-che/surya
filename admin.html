⚠️ Video Upload Recommendations

For the best website performance and visitor experience, please follow these guidelines:

  • Upload videos only when necessary.
  • Images create a stronger and more engaging photography portfolio.
  • Recommended video length: up to 4 minutes.
  • Recommended maximum size: 150 MB (MP4 - H.264).

Very large videos can increase upload time, consume more storage and bandwidth, and may result in slower loading for visitors, especially on mobile networks.

If your video is larger than 150 MB, please compress it before uploading using a tool such as HandBrake.

📸 We recommend showcasing your work primarily with high-quality images. Images load much faster, create a cleaner portfolio, and usually leave a stronger first impression on potential clients. Use videos only for your best highlight moments.
